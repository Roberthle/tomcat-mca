Why UCC equipment finance leads convert higher

The Power of UCC Filings

Brokers consistently find that UCC equipment finance leads convert at a higher rate. A Uniform Commercial Code (UCC) filing indicates that a business has previously financed equipment, showing they understand the process and have a proven need for capital.

Benefits of Targeting UCC Equipment Finance Leads

These leads are highly targeted. By analyzing UCC data, you can identify businesses that are likely nearing the end of their lease or finance terms, making them prime candidates for refinancing or new equipment purchases.

  • Proven Borrowing History: The business has a track record of securing financing.
  • Clear Intent: Existing filings often reveal the exact type of equipment the business uses.

Maximizing Your ROI

To capitalize on UCC equipment finance leads, tailor your pitch to their specific industry needs and highlight how your rates or terms outperform their current financing.
